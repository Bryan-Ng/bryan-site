PocketRx

Pocket reference for pharmacists.


Co-created with Joo Hwan Oh
Community pharmacists can improve patient outcomes by asking pointed questions.
Specific questions open up conversation to assess patients and identify gaps in care.
PocketRx is a quick reference that provides framework for pharmacists assess patients.

PocketRx is a tool to aid clinicians in exercising their clinical skills. It is not medical advice and does not replace clinical knowledge. Feb 2020 - Present.
