Academic Papers

A systematic review of morbidities suggestive of the multiple sclerosis prodrome

Systematic Review.

Twenty-nine studies were included. Anxiety, depression, migraine, and lower cognitive performance may be a part of the MS prodrome.

Published March 21, 2020.

Effects of long-term inhaled corticosteroid treatment on fragility fractures in older women: the Manitoba BMD registry study.

Retrospective Cohort Study.

Findings do not support an increased risk of major osteoporotic fracture in older women with Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease and/or Asthma who use inhaled corticosteroids.

Published March 02, 2020.

Student-run clinic association: the next generation of health system collaboration.

Commentary.

Discusses the Student-Run Clinic Association as a platform for student-run clinics across Canada to collaborate and improve care to underserved populations.

Published December 28, 2019.

The direct costs of overdiagnosed asthma in a longitudinal population-based study.

Longitudinal population-based study.

Approximately one-quarter of patients with a previous diagnosis of asthma currently do not have asthma. The economic burden of overdiagnosed asthma may be significant.

Published November 07, 2019.
